India's EPR Market Is Growing Fast

India's Plastic Waste Management Rules 2022 mandate EPR compliance for every PIBO (Producer, Importer, Brand Owner). With 200,000+ companies now legally required to get EPR certificates — and most of them still non-compliant — the market need is massive.

Penalties are rising. CPCB enforcement is intensifying. And companies are actively looking for trusted EPR partners in their cities and states.

As a Pro India franchisee, you become the trusted EPR compliance partner in your state territory — backed by a CPCB-registered PRO, a 10-year-old brand, and a pan-India recycling network that companies cannot build on their own.

Franchise FAQ

What does the Pro India EPR franchise include?

The franchise includes: state-level exclusive territory, Pro India's CPCB-registered PRO status, complete EPR compliance training, access to the recycling partner network, digital platform for tracking and filing, co-branded marketing materials, and ongoing mentorship from the central team.

What is the investment required?

Franchise investment varies by state territory. Contact Pro India to receive the current franchise kit with full investment details, projected revenue, and payback timelines. A free discovery call is the first step — no commitment required.

Do I need prior experience in recycling or sustainability?

No. Pro India provides complete training. A background in business, B2B sales, or operations is helpful but not mandatory. Our best-performing franchisees come from diverse backgrounds including manufacturing, distribution, and social enterprise.

How long does the onboarding take?

Onboarding takes approximately 4 weeks from signing. This includes EPR compliance training, platform access, recycler network introduction, and the first client acquisition support cycle.

Can I hold a franchise for multiple states?

Yes, after successfully running your first state territory for a minimum period. Multi-state expansions are common among our top-performing franchisees. This is discussed during the discovery call.
