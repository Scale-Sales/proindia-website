Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Pro India ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to proindia.net and related landing pages, forms, WhatsApp and email communications.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email, phone number, company and message — when you submit a form, request a consultation, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us via WhatsApp, call or email. We also collect usage data automatically (pages visited, time on site, device and approximate location) through cookies and analytics tools including Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel and Microsoft Clarity.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to respond to enquiries, provide our services, send you relevant updates and marketing (where permitted), improve our website and campaigns, and comply with legal obligations. Leads submitted through our website are recorded in our CRM (Zoho) and may be included in newsletters and WhatsApp or email campaigns, from which you can unsubscribe at any time.

3. Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, remarketing and personalisation. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted service providers (e.g. CRM, email, WhatsApp and analytics platforms) who process it on our behalf, and with authorities where required by law.

5. Data retention & security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law, and we apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and may opt out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Contact us

For any privacy queries, email info@proindia.net or call +91-9319001214.

This policy is provided for general information and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before publication.
