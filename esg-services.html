FAQ

ESG Services — Frequently Asked Questions

What ESG services does Pro India offer?

ESG consultancy (net-zero, footprinting, zero waste), ESG audits and gap closure (Scope 1/2/3 carbon, water and waste), ESG reporting and assurance (BRSR, Sustainability Reports, EPD, CBAM), and corporate ESG action training towards net zero.

Is BRSR reporting mandatory in India?

BRSR is mandatory for the top 1,000 listed companies by market capitalisation, as mandated by SEBI. BRSR Core assurance requirements are being phased in, including for value chains.

What is CBAM and does it affect Indian exporters?

The EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism puts a carbon price on imports of carbon-intensive goods like steel, aluminium, cement and fertilizers. Indian exporters to the EU must report embedded emissions and will face charges — making carbon accounting essential.

What are Scope 1, 2 and 3 emissions?

Scope 1 is direct emissions from owned sources, Scope 2 is indirect emissions from purchased energy, and Scope 3 is all other value-chain emissions. We measure and help reduce all three as part of a net-zero roadmap.

Do you serve MSMEs as well as large corporates?

Yes. Large corporates typically engage the full ESG suite; MSMEs (₹50–500 Cr) often start with supply-chain sustainability and BRSR value-chain readiness — see our MSME Support page.
