MSME Support — Frequently Asked Questions

Why do MSMEs need sustainability now?

OEMs, large customers and exporters increasingly require suppliers to demonstrate sustainability, safety and business-continuity credentials. For MSMEs in the ₹50–500 crore range, meeting these demands is now a condition of winning and keeping business, and of accessing green finance.

What is the SCALE program?

SCALE is Pro India's growth program for CEOs and owners. Over 6–12 months, Amit Saha coaches and mentors the top management on sales growth, operational excellence and business transformation — drawing on 30 years of P&L and turnaround experience.

Does Pro India offer franchises?

Yes. Pro India offers state-level franchises for entrepreneurs to build plastic-recycling and EPR compliance businesses under the Pro India brand, with a standardised playbook, training and a single-window partner network. See our Franchise page.

How is this different from generic consulting?

It's practitioner-led. Amit has owned P&Ls, run 11+ factories and turned around businesses — so the advice is grounded in operating reality, not slides. Engagements are structured around measurable growth and ROI.
